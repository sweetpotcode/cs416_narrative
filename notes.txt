Here's how to publish cs416_narrative on GitHub Pages

    Go to your repo:

        https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/cs416_narrative

    Click “Settings” → “Pages” (in the sidebar)

    Under “GitHub Pages” section:

        Source: Choose main (or your default branch)

        Folder: Choose / (root) (not /docs)

        Click Save

    After a few seconds, GitHub will give you a URL like:

        https://YOUR_USERNAME.github.io/cs416_narrative/
